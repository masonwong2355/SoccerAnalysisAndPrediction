Data Collections

Datasets is collected and provided by Wyscout , which have a proprietary software and expert video analysts, who are focused on data collection for football. They released free datasets on figshare for people views and analysts. The datasets include five first division national scoccer competitions, which include Spanish, Italian, English, German and French, in season 2017/2018. Also, World cup 2018 and the European cup 2016 are provided.
